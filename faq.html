Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our commercial kitchen equipment repair services in Seattle and surrounding areas.

General Service

Basic questions about our repair services

What commercial equipment do you repair? +
We specialize in BUNN coffee machines, TurboChef ovens, Schaerer espresso machines, SureShot dairy systems, and other commercial kitchen equipment. Our technicians are trained on multiple brands and models.
How quickly can you repair my equipment? +
We offer same-day service in most cases. Our technicians carry common parts and diagnostic tools, allowing us to complete many repairs during the initial visit. Emergency repairs are available.
What areas do you service? +
We serve Seattle, Bellevue, Tacoma, Renton, Kent, Federal Way, Bothell, Redmond, Kirkland, Issaquah, and all of King County. Other areas are available - call to inquire about your location.

Equipment Specific

Questions about specific equipment brands

Do you offer TurboChef drop-off service? +
Yes! We offer drop-off service for TurboChef ovens. This allows for faster repairs without travel charges and is often more cost-effective for non-urgent repairs.
What BUNN coffee machine models do you service? +
We service all BUNN commercial coffee machines including brewers, grinders, and dispensers. Our technicians are experienced with both older and newer models.
Can you repair Schaerer espresso machines? +
Absolutely. We provide complete repair services for Schaerer espresso machines and other commercial coffee equipment, including maintenance and part replacement.

Pricing & Parts

Cost and parts information

How much does equipment repair cost? +
Repair costs depend on the specific issue and required parts. We provide upfront estimates after diagnosing the problem and always get approval before starting work.
Do you use genuine parts? +
Yes, we use only genuine OEM parts to ensure optimal performance and maintain equipment warranties. We stock common components for faster repairs.
Do you charge for diagnostic time? +
We provide transparent pricing for all services including diagnostic time. All costs are discussed upfront with no hidden fees.

Service Process

How our repair process works

What happens during a service call? +
Our technician arrives with diagnostic tools and common parts, performs a thorough inspection, provides an estimate, completes the repair with your approval, and tests the equipment thoroughly.
Do you test equipment after repairs? +
Yes, we perform complete testing of all equipment functionality to ensure proper operation before considering the job complete.
How do I schedule a repair? +
Simply call us at 425-547-0200 or email services@fixitexpresso.com. We'll gather equipment details, discuss the issue, and schedule service at your convenience.
