TurboChef Not Heating

Symptoms: Unit powers on but food doesn't cook, cold cooking chamber

Common Causes: Magnetron failure, blown fuses, faulty door sensors, control board issues

Quick Check: Ensure door seals properly, check for error codes on display

Professional repair required for magnetron and electrical components.

TurboChef Error Codes

F1: Blower Running Status Bad

F2: Cook Temperature Low

F3: Magnetron Current Low

F4: Door Monitor Defective

F5: Magnetron Over Temperature

F6: EC Temp High

Call 425-547-0200 for error code diagnosis.

TurboChef Door Problems

Issues: Door won't close properly, unit won't start, heating problems

Causes: Worn door seals, misaligned hinges, faulty door sensors

Check: Clean door seals, ensure door closes completely

Professional seal replacement and sensor repair available.

TurboChef Power Issues

Problems: Unit won't power on, frequent breaker trips, display issues

Causes: Power supply problems, control board failure, wiring issues

Safety: Never attempt electrical repairs yourself

Electrical diagnosis and repair available.
