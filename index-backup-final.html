Commercial Equipment Repair FAQ

What commercial equipment do you repair?

We repair BUNN coffee machines, TurboChef ovens, Schaerer espresso machines, SureShot dairy systems, and other commercial kitchen equipment. Our technicians are trained on all major brands and models.

How quickly can you repair my commercial equipment?

We offer fast repair service for most issues. Our technicians carry common parts and can often complete repairs during the initial visit. Emergency service is available for critical kitchen operations.

Do you use genuine parts?

Yes, we stock and use only genuine OEM parts to ensure optimal performance and maintain your warranty. We maintain an inventory of common components for faster repair completion.

What areas do you service?

We provide commercial equipment repair throughout Seattle, Bellevue, Tacoma, Renton, and King County. Other areas may be available depending on our availability - please call to discuss your location. We also offer drop-off service for TurboChef ovens with no travel charges.

How much does equipment repair cost?

Repair costs are determined after diagnosing the specific issue and identifying required parts. We provide upfront estimates before starting any work so there are no surprises.

Do you offer drop-off service?

Yes! Starting this year, we offer drop-off service for TurboChef ovens. This allows for faster repairs and waives travel charges, making it a convenient and cost-effective option for TurboChef oven repairs.
