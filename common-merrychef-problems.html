MerryChef Oven Error Codes & Initial Troubleshooting

Customer-friendly checks for MerryChef eikon and conneX speed ovens — try these before scheduling service

MerryChef Error Codes

Codes you can often resolve yourself are shown first with simple checks. Other codes need a technician — call us with the code displayed and we'll arrive prepared.

Codes You Can Try to Resolve Yourself

E103 – Oven Overheating

Most Common

What it means

The electronics compartment has overheated, often due to a dirty air filter.

Initial Checks

  • Turn the oven off and allow it to cool for 30 minutes.
  • Remove and clean the air filter.
  • Make sure nothing is blocking ventilation openings.
  • Confirm the oven is not installed too close to other heat-producing equipment.
Call for service if: The error returns after cleaning and cooling.

E104 – Magnetron / Cavity Overheat

What it means

The microwave system or cooking cavity has overheated.

Initial Checks

  • Turn the oven off immediately.
  • Clean the air filter thoroughly.
  • Allow the oven to cool for at least one hour.
  • Restart the oven.
Call for service if: The error remains after cooling.

E105 – Power Supply Issue

What it means

Incoming voltage is too high or too low.

Initial Checks

  • Verify the oven is plugged into its dedicated circuit.
  • Check for tripped breakers.
  • Turn the oven off for 5 minutes and restart.
Call for service if: The error continues.

E107 – Internal Communication Error

What it means

The oven's control boards are unable to communicate properly.

Initial Checks

  • Turn power off for 5 minutes.
  • Restart the oven.
Call for service if: The error returns.

E115 – Convection Fan Failure

What it means

The main cooking fan is not operating correctly.

Initial Checks

  • Restart the oven.
  • Listen for the fan during startup.
  • Check for unusual noises.
Call for service if: The fan does not run or the error returns.

E117 – Magnetron Overheat

What it means

The microwave system has overheated and requires cooling.

Initial Checks

  • Allow the oven to cool completely.
  • Clean both air filters.
  • Ensure adequate ventilation around the oven.
Call for service if: The error keeps returning.

Codes That Need a Technician

These codes indicate internal faults that aren't safe for operators to resolve. Note the code, then call FixIt Expresso so we can arrive with the right parts.

E101 – Microwave System Failure

What it means

The oven's microwave system failed to start.

Action: Stop using the oven and contact FixIt Expresso.

E102 – Heater Current Fault

What it means

The heating element is drawing incorrect current.

Action: Contact FixIt Expresso for diagnosis.

E106 – Cavity Temperature Too High

What it means

The oven temperature has exceeded its safe operating range.

Action: Allow the oven to cool, then contact FixIt Expresso if it persists.

E108 – Personality Module / SD Card Error

What it means

The oven cannot read its configuration module or SD card.

Action: Contact FixIt Expresso for diagnosis.

E109 – Personality Module Failure

What it means

The oven's personality module is missing or invalid.

Action: Contact FixIt Expresso for diagnosis.

E110 – Firmware Version Conflict

What it means

Internal control boards have incompatible software versions.

Action: Contact FixIt Expresso to align firmware.

E111 – Cavity Temperature Sensor Error

What it means

The oven cannot read the cavity temperature sensor.

Action: Contact FixIt Expresso for diagnosis.

E113 – Microwave Safety Fault

What it means

The magnetron remained energized when it should have turned off.

Action: Stop using the oven immediately and contact FixIt Expresso.

E116 – Heater Failure

What it means

The heating element failed to turn on.

Action: Contact FixIt Expresso for diagnosis.

Common Problems Without Error Codes

Oven Will Not Turn On

  • Verify power outlet is working.
  • Check circuit breaker.
  • Unplug for 5 minutes and reconnect.

"Door Open" Message When Door Is Closed

  • Check for debris around the door.
  • Inspect door alignment.
  • Open and close the door firmly.

Oven Runs But Food Is Not Heating

  • Verify the correct cooking program is selected.
  • Clean the oven cavity.
  • Check for any stored error codes.

Food Not Crisping Properly

  • Clean impinger plates.
  • Clean grease filters.
  • Verify correct cooking profile is being used.

⚠️ Important Safety Notice

MerryChef ovens contain high-voltage electrical components and microwave radiation systems. Never attempt to repair internal components yourself. Always disconnect power before any inspection and contact certified technicians for proper diagnosis and repair.
