TurboChef Error Codes (F1–F10)

If your TurboChef is showing one of these codes, note it down and call us with the code and your oven model — we'll arrive prepared.

F1 – Blower Running Status Bad

Motor or power issue.

F2 – Cook Temperature Low

Oven won't reach setpoint.

F3 – Magnetron Current Low

Microwave circuit failure.

F4 – Door Monitor Defective

Door switch or interlock problem.

F5 – Magnetron Over-Temperature

Usually a clogged vent.

F6 – EC Temp High

Restricted airflow somewhere.

F7 – RTD Failure

Temperature probe issue (display often shows 999).

F8 – High Limit Tripped / Heat Low

Breaker or component issue.

F9 – Belt Failure / Temp High

Grease buildup or belt issue.

F10 – Communication Failure

UI or I/O board issue.
